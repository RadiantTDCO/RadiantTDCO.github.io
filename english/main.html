Welcome to TokonTo

Welcome to the TokonTo subject page from Panyaprateep School.

Our school isn’t just about getting good grades; it’s about learning things that truly matter in life. TokonTo is a unique subject where we explore who we are, prepare for the future, and discover our passions.

In Grade 9, we face important decisions—choosing the right subjects for next year and envisioning our future careers. TokonTo helps us navigate these challenges by introducing us to various career paths, revealing our strengths, and igniting our curiosity.

This website is part of my knowledge management project, designed to showcase the transformative experiences in TokonTo. Enjoy exploring the journey, and let it inspire you to grow and discover new possibilities!
